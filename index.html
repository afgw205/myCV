Academic Projects


Masters Dissertation
Comparison of Outlier Detection Methods in High Dimensional Data: ADD and RDOF Methods

  • No previous study had thoroughly examined performance of novel average divergence difference-based (ADD) and Relative Density-Based Outlier Factor (RDOF) outlier detection methods in high dimensional data.
  • This paper proposed to evaluate the outlier detection performance in several settings of high dimensional data and percentages of outliers in datasets.
  • This paper proposed new scale measurement of high dimensionality of datasets with the concept of gap between the number of variables and observations.


Degree Final Year Project
Forecasting of Covid-19 Cases and Study of the Effectiveness of Movement Control Order (MCO) Phases Implementation in Malaysia

  • Fatal cases attributed to COVID-19 had been the main concern worldwide after the pandemic breakout.
  • This paper aimed to determine the best forecasting model to predict death cases in Malaysia
  • This paper aimed to determine the effectiveness of Movement Control Order (MCO) implemented to curb the spread of COVID-19 virus in Malaysia.
